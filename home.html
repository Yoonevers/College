Welcome to GPREC

Welcome to G. Pulla Reddy Engineering College (GPREC), a beacon of excellence in engineering education. Accredited by NBA and NAAC with an ‘A’ grade, GPREC is renowned for its academic achievements, cutting-edge research, and strong industry collaborations. Our students consistently excel in competitive exams and secure placements in top global companies. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a focus on holistic development, GPREC nurtures future leaders and innovators ready to make a significant impact on society.
